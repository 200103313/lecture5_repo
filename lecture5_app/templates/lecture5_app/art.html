Art in Kazakhstan

In the culture of the Sakas, Huns and Usuns, there are images of real and fantastic animals (the so-called "animal style").
In the objects of material culture of the Middle Ages, there was a painting of clay vessels, weapons, horse utensils.
Ch. Valikhanov was the first Kazakh professional painter. Traveling a lot, studying the history and culture of the
Kazakhs and Kirghiz, in his diaries he made sketches of people and nature that were distinguished by the brightness
of life observations.
